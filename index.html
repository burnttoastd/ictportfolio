Who I Am

I am Ralph, a student at Anglo-Chinese Secondary School (Barker Road). Since I was young, I have been fascinated by various aspects of IT, including hardware in desktops, where I have learned about different processing units and how they work together to prevent system bottlenecks. Subsequently, I shifted my attention from hardware to software development, diving into coding. I attended formal coding lessons and then transitioned to self-directed online learning. What caught my eye was Cyber Security, as I had a personal experience with it at thirteen when my Discord account was compromised. I felt violated and angry because I had to close my account and start a new one. This stressful incident sparked my interest in malware, its various types, and the severe harm malware can cause to computer systems. I began questioning how I can prevent this type of malware from infiltrating my computer. From then onwards, I have been exploring the intricacies of computer systems, the complexities of malware threats and preventive strategies. I am passionate about the dynamic field of technological security and its legal implications, which enable us to be better protected from cyber threats.
